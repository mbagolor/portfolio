Brochure

ART 120–Intro to Typography

In this project, we had the freedom to choose a subject or real-life exhibition to design and create a Museum Brochure. I chose Jeff Koons’s artwork and created a tri-fold brochure. By gathering images, copywriting, choosing the right typography, and the style of the brochure I was able to create a design that fits the artist’s work.

Service: Typesetting, Print Design
